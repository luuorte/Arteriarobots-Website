Arteria robots prototype

    The robot that unclogs arteries

  • What about our prototype?

    • With this robot, what you were doing is revolutionizing the health market, through a probe this robot travels through the circulatory system, and it will be able to detect through its infrared sensor and its microcamera, when an artery is clogged, which will make it unclog the arteries with its mini claw, one by one, once an artery is unclogged, this robot will send a notification to the computer, iPad, or cell phone where the patient can see their artery decongestion process

    For the safety of the patient, this procedure will have to be performed in the hospital and the patient will have to be monitored in the room by an angiologist, a specialist in diseases in the circulatory system.
